My Favourite Discs

The Innova Firebird is an overstable fairway driver which works extremely well into a headwind. This disc finds it's place in everyone's bag.

The Tilt from Discmania is known as the most overstable driver in disc golf. Designed by Simon Lizotte, the Tilt comes in the new Meta plastic. This is a great unique, fun, and get out of trouble disc

The Ozone is a flippy fairway driver designed for the fun line. Whether it's the satisfyingly straight shot or the all-too-ridiculous-sky-anny, remember simply to enjoy it.

The Svea is an understable mid range by Kastaplast. The Svea has a ton of glide and can handle low power to get a little extra distance.
